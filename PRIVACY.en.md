# CinemaGazer Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-15

The CinemaGazer Chrome extension (the "Extension") respects your privacy.

## Information We Collect

The Extension **does not collect any personal information**.

The information the Extension accesses is limited to the following:

- **Subtitle timing information of the supported streaming services (Netflix / Hulu / Disney+ / Amazon Prime Video)**: The Extension reads the subtitle files (TTML/VTT) that the player loads — at the fetch/XMLHttpRequest level — or observes the on-screen subtitle rendering, in order to extract the display intervals (start/end times) of subtitles. This is necessary to switch the playback rate between subtitled and non-speech parts.
- **User settings**: Settings such as playback rates, minimum non-speech gap, and overlay visibility are saved to `chrome.storage.sync`. This is Chrome's standard storage and syncs across the user's Google account.

## How Information Is Handled

All of the above is **processed entirely within the user's browser**. The Extension performs **no** transmission to external servers, no provision to third parties, and no persistent storage (other than the settings).

## Third Parties

The Extension performs **no** tracking, analytics, advertising, or communication with external APIs.

## Security

The Extension complies with Manifest V3 and requests only the minimum necessary permissions.

## Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as features are added or legal requirements change. When there is a significant change, the last-updated date will be revised together with a version update of the Extension.
